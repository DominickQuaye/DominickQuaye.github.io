VANS RV-10 4 Seater Airplane

I am currently working as a shop assistant for Alex Chan building a VANS RV-10 Airplane. We have already finished the elevator, rudder, tailcone, and tail, and are now about halfway through building the fuselage. The plane's build has far too many steps and components to go over everything I have worked on so far, so I am just going to present the work as a photo gallery of each section of the plane as the build progresses. In working this job I have learned a number of new machining skills, as well as better quality control and ANSI military standards.
